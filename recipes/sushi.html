Sushi Recipe

Description

Super simple and easy (but tasty) sushi that the kids can help on!

Ingredients

For the rice:

  • 300g sushi rice
  • 100ml rice wine vinegar
  • 2 tbsp golden caster sugar

For the japanese mayonnaise

  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp soy sauce

For the sushi

  • 25g bag nori (seaweed) sheets
  • Any fillings:
    • cucumber strips
    • smoked salmon
    • white crabmeat
    • canned tuna
    • red pepper
    • avocado
    • spring onion

Extras to serve with

  • wasabi (fiery)
  • pickled ginger
  • soy sauce

Method

    Part 1: Androstenedione

    Note: If you already have androstenedione, part 1 is not a necessary step.

  1. First to synthesis everything you have to start with cholesterol and convert it into 20α,22R-dihydroxycholesterol (oxidise it a few times)
  2. Next convert the 20α,22R-dihydroxycholesterol you have into pregnenolone, catalysing the reaction using cytochrome P450scc.
  3. Once you have pregenelone it is pretty easy to get progesterone, some 3β-hydroxysteroid dehydrogenase/δ5-4-isomerase will catalyse that reaction nicely.
  4. Use that fresh progesterone and metabolise it to 17 alpha-hydroxysteroids by a cytochrome P450-dependent 17 aplha hydroxylase (P450c17) enzyme
  5. Now we can reuse that enxyme to catalyse the removal of the side chain of these 17 alpha-hydroxolated steroids to yield some androstenedione (and dehydroepiandrosterone)
  6. Androstenedione obtained!

    Part 2: Estrogen

    Note: If you already have estrogen, part 1 and 2 are not necessary steps.

  1. Estrogen is very easy to make. Simply use the enzyme aromatase to convert your androstenedione into estrone
  2. Estrone is a form of estrogen but for the most feminine effect on the song you must convert it to estriadol
  3. To get estriadol from estrone you must convert it using 17β-hydroxysteroid dehydrogenase (17β-HSD).
  4. You can also produce estriadol from testosterone, by cycling it through a human circulatory system, some of it will be converted into estriadol
  5. Estrogen obtained!

    6. PS: In case you have no testosterone you can convert androstenedione into testosterone using the same enzyme: (17β-HSD).

    Part 3: The song

  1. To make the beat set your keyboard into E key and mash together some keys until they sound good, play around a bit!
  2. Make sure to add loads of synth. If you think it's too much. Add more.
  3. Next add some random normie noises like some drums, don't worry the reverb will make the sound indiscernable but the unusual instrument - in drain at least- will make you edgy and unique.
  4. The penultimate step is to make everything reverb. Make it ricochet and bounce off the walls.
  5. Finally the SINGLE most important step in making this #draingang song into anyhting remotely like Amygdala, inject the estrogen (estriadol).
